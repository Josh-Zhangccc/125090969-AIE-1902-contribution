# University Sports Hall

Featuring a comprehensive range of sports facilities: badminton courts, table tennis rooms, taekwondo training area, fitness studios, indoor heated swimming pool, bouldering gym, fitness center, track and field stadium, tennis courts, you name it!


Additionally, the Sports Department hosts exciting events every year, such as the "Freshmen Cup" Tournament, Campus Marathon, Color Run,etc...

Even the President, professors, and college tutors join in — sports truly bring the whole campus together!
