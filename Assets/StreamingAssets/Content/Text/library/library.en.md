# Library

This building shaped like an open book is  the Library.

Home to 180,000 physical books, 20,000+ e-books, and nearly 300 online databases — CNKI and all academic resources are free to access and download!


- 🎬 **Editing Zone**: Ground floor dual-screen computers for group video editing — productivity MAX!
- 💻 **All iMacs**: Talk is cheap, but actually coding and working on projects with free Macs in the library? *Smirking face*!
- 📚 **Top Publisher Collections**: Princeton, Oxford, Cambridge original English literature at your fingertips.
- 🤝 **Group Study Rooms**: Book online, big screens + soundproof design — study in peace!
