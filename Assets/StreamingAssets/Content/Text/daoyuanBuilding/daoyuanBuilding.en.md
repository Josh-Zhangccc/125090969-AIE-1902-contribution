# Dao Yuan Building & Neighboring Buildings

- **Dao Yuan Building**  
  Dao Yuan Building stands as CUHK-SZ's premier academic hub, housing the prestigious Council Chamber equipped with state-of-the-art multimedia systems. This iconic building frequently hosts high-level academic forums, distinguished guest lectures, and pivotal university meetings. It has witnessed countless milestone decisions that shape the university's future, embodying the institution's commitment to excellence and innovation.

- **Zhi Xin Building**  
  One of the campus's earliest landmarks, Zhi Xin Building carries rich historical significance. It primarily accommodates foundational course classrooms and essential academic facilities, welcoming generations of students at the start of their academic journey. Its classic architectural design blends seamlessly with modern teaching technology, creating a timeless learning environment.

- **Cheng Dao Building**  
  Designed for interactive education, Cheng Dao Building specializes in collaborative learning spaces. It features flexible discussion rooms, small-group seminar halls, and project-based learning labs. The building's open layout encourages spontaneous academic exchanges, making it a favorite spot for group projects and interdisciplinary discussions.

- **Zhi Ren Building**  
  Dedicated to fostering cross-disciplinary innovation, Zhi Ren Building serves as a dynamic hub for academic exploration. Its versatile seminar rooms and workshop spaces host cutting-edge research workshops, innovation bootcamps, and interdisciplinary lectures. The building's design promotes knowledge fusion across different fields.

- **Le Tian Building**  
  Le Tian Building showcases modern educational architecture with its advanced lecture halls and experimental teaching zones. Equipped with smart classroom technology and flexible seating arrangements, it supports innovative teaching methodologies like flipped classrooms and interactive lectures. The building also houses specialized labs for hands-on learning experiences.

- **Henry and Vivien Wong Library**  
  Named in honor of its generous benefactors, this main library is the intellectual heart of the campus. Boasting an extensive collection of over 180,000 physical books, 20,000+ e-books, and nearly 300 online databases, it offers comprehensive academic resources. The library's diverse study zones, from quiet reading areas to collaborative spaces, cater to every learning style.
