# Research Building

The Research Building is CUHK-SZ's epicenter of cutting-edge scientific exploration and innovation. This state-of-the-art facility brings together researchers and students from diverse disciplines to tackle global challenges through collaborative discovery.

🔬 **Key Features:**

- **Advanced Laboratories**: Equipped with world-class equipment for fields including artificial intelligence, biotechnology, materials science, and quantum computing
- **Nobel Prize-Winning Labs**: Home to research centers led by Nobel laureates like Professor Arieh Warshel and Professor Brian Kobilka, driving groundbreaking discoveries
- **Interdisciplinary Research Hubs**: Dedicated spaces fostering cross-pollination between AI, data science, medicine, and engineering
- **Innovation Incubator**: Supporting student startups and research commercialization with mentorship and resources
- **Collaborative Spaces**: Open-concept work areas and meeting rooms designed to spark creative thinking and knowledge exchange

The Research Building represents CUHK-SZ's commitment to pushing the boundaries of knowledge and transforming groundbreaking ideas into real-world solutions.
