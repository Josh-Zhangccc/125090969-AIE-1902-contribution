# Concert Hall/Liwen Hall

The Grand Event Auditoriom

This is the most sacred ritual ground of CUHK-SZ — where opening ceremonies, graduation ceremonies, freshmen balls... almost every major campus event takes place!

The most famous is the annual "Autumn Gala" — a campus spectacle comparable to the Spring Festival Gala! The President presides in person, deans from all colleges gather, and even friends from Shatian Campus travel through to join the Mid-Autumn carnival night!
