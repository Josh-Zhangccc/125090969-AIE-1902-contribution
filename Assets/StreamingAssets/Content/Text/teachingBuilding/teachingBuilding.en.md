# Teaching Buildings

To the left of the avenue lies the teaching building complex, including the Teaching Complex and Teaching Buildings A, B, C, D.

Courses from each school (Management and Economics, Science and Engineering, Data Science, Humanities and Social Science, Music, Medicine, Public Policy (Graduate-only), and Artificial Intelligence) are distributed across these buildings.

No homeroom teachers or administrative classes here! You might end up taking the same interesting course with students from other majors! Inter-school socialing barriers breaking!

Under Teaching D, you'll find student-founded brands like "Yi Ou Cha", plus startups like "Huiyin Coffee" and "Three Chairs Barbershop" — proof of CUHK-SZ's support for student entrepreneurship. Next to it is Tu Huilong Building, which houses Nobel Prize-winning labs like Wacker and Kobilka, showcasing the university's impressive research strength.
